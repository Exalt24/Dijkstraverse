How to Use

  • 🖱️Click any empty cell to toggle a wall.
  • 🎯Drag the green (start) and red (end) cells—only onto non-wall cells.
  • ▶️Hit "Find Path" to see the algorithm in action.
  • ⚖️Use randomizers to shuffle start/end, walls, or weights.
  • 🌀Generate a maze or save/load your layout.
